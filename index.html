‚óÜ Main Components
Bone-in short ribs (4-6) - alternative: anything with collagen who cares
1 tbsp ground pepper - a lot is what matters not the volume
2 tbsp crushed peppercorns - it's more pepper! grind it if you want
Like 8 cloves garlic - most of a head, just use a fuckton
1-2 tbsp tomato paste
Chianti or similar wine - 2 cups worth
‚óÜ Herbs & Aromatics
Sage
Thyme
Bay leaves
Optional vegetables: pearl onions, carrot, celery, onion, bell pepper, potato
Add them at the right time, not the wrong one. What the right time is is left as an exercise to the reader.
‚óÜ Directions
1 Salt short ribs. You may sear them but need not. I sometimes sear them and use the fond for something else entirely, then use the seared ribs with a fondless pot. Chaos.
2 Turn the garlic into a paste using salt and a mortar and pestle. This is important.
3 Rub short ribs with garlic paste and tomato paste. Get messy.
4 In a tightly covered dutch oven, braise in 2 cups wine with all the herbs n shit, about 3.5 hours, turning every 30 mins or so. Set timers or forget and panic.
5 Serve with polenta or mashed potato or something. Top with parsnip chips if you're feeling fancy.
